Editorial Note

The $10,000 threshold is a single-building signal intended to flag persistent non-payment of civil penalties rather than routine small-balance situations. A future version of this rule will evaluate outstanding balances across an actor's full portfolio, which is a stronger evasion signal. ECB balance data reflects OATH records and may not capture payments made after the last data refresh.
