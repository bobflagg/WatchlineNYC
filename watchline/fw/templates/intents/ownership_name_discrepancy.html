Important Limitation — Read Before Citing This Finding

The resolved controller's name is the single most frequently registered contact name across that Actor's entire multi-building portfolio, not necessarily the specific name this individual building was registered under with HPD. Verified against live data: among buildings where DOF's owner name is not corporate-looking, 71% show no name overlap with the portfolio's dominant name — a rate too high to be read as evidence of concealment on its own. Treat a positive OND-001 flag as a lead worth checking by hand, not a finding. Corporate-looking DOF owner names (LLC, INC, CORP, etc.) are excluded from evaluation entirely, since an LLC holding title while a natural person controls it is the expected, non-suspicious shape of most NYC rental ownership.
