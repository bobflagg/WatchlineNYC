Data Limitations

Court action data is sourced from HPD litigations only. OCA (Office of Court Administration) housing court data, which would capture filings by tenants and other parties, has not yet been ingested into the Watchline graph. HPD also enforces through the Alternative Enforcement Program (AEP) and Emergency Repair Program (ERP), which are not captured as court filings. A zero court action count reflects the HPD litigations record, not necessarily the complete enforcement history.
