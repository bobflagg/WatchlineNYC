Methodological Note

The multi-year signal uses the year of the oldest currently-open Class C violation as a proxy for years with persistent conditions. It does not verify that Class C violations were continuously open in each intervening year, only that at least one has remained open since the threshold year. A violation opened in 2022 and still open in 2026 counts as 4 years, but the building may have had periods with fewer active violations in between. This is a conservative approximation.
