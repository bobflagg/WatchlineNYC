Editorial Note

The rs_deregulating flag is derived from Furman Center analysis of DHCR records. Watchline does not independently compute this flag. A building can show a negative unit change due to legitimate vacancies, rehabilitation exemptions, or data corrections. Requiring the deregistration flag is intended to distinguish systematic removal of units from the stabilization registry from transient fluctuations. Both signals must be satisfied for the rule to fire.
