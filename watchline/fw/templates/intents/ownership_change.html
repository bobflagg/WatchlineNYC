Data Limitations
ACRIS coverage starts 2010. Deed transfers before 2010 are not in the graph. Buildings sold before 2010 will show a shorter before-period than their actual history, which may understate the pre-transfer violation rate and produce false negatives.

Arm's-length filter. Trust transfers ($0 deeds), intra-family transfers, and partial interest sales (< 50% transferred) are excluded. The filter is conservative: some legitimate sales involve below-market prices (e.g. family sales, estate transfers) that would be excluded.

Rate comparison does not account for building size. A building with 100 units will naturally have higher violation counts than one with 10 units. A future version should normalize by residential unit count.
