Connection Type Breakdown

Watchline assembles ownership networks using two types of HPD registration connections: name-based (same registrant name across buildings) and address-based (same registered business address across buildings). A portfolio dominated by address-based connections indicates that buildings are held under different entity names at a shared address — a structural pattern associated with LLC fragmentation. Source: HPD registration data via Watchline portfolio pipeline.

Epistemic Limitations

A high address-to-name ratio is a necessary but not sufficient indicator of concealment. Legitimate portfolio structures may also use shared business addresses (e.g., a property management company's office) across buildings held by related but independently-operating entities. The signal requires investigation, not presumption. The observed entity names shown are raw strings from HPD registration records and may contain transcription variants or errors.
