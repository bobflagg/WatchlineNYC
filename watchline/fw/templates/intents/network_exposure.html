Epistemic note: ProbableAffiliation vs BeneficialControl

ProbableAffiliation (NE-001) is a weaker inference than ProbableBeneficialControl (PBC-001). BeneficialControl is asserted when two registrations share enough direct connections (shared addresses or names) to be placed in the same ownership network. ProbableAffiliation is asserted when two separate ownership networks share structural origin in the same Weakly Connected Component — meaning they were connected indirectly, through intermediary registrations, before community detection divided them. The combined portfolio shown in this report should be treated as an investigative starting point, not as a proven ownership structure. Each actor's individual portfolio is supported by PBC-001; the connection between actors is supported only by NE-001.
