Ranking Methodology

This ranking applies no new rule. It aggregates pre-computed findings from Rule PHC-001 (Persistent Hazardous Conditions, RUL-00001) across each landlord's probable beneficial control portfolio (Rule PBC-001, RUL-00002). Rank is determined by raw PHC building count, not PHC rate — a large landlord with many PHC buildings ranks higher than a small landlord with a 100% PHC rate. Both metrics are shown so the reader can weight them appropriately.

Minimum Portfolio Filter

Landlords with fewer than 5 buildings in the Watchline graph are excluded. Small portfolios often satisfy PHC-001 on all buildings by coincidence of scale rather than systemic neglect.

Epistemic Limitations

Portfolio boundaries are determined by Rule PBC-001, which infers probable beneficial control from HPD registration patterns. Actors whose portfolios are misidentified or split across multiple network nodes will appear with incomplete counts. This ranking reflects the state of the Watchline graph at last build and should be treated as a starting point for investigation, not a definitive finding.
