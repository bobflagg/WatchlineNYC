PHC Buildings by Borough

Buildings satisfying Rule PHC-001 (Persistent Hazardous Conditions), grouped by borough. PHC-001 fires when a building has 3 or more open Class C (immediately hazardous) violations with the oldest open for more than 180 days. Source: Watchline epistemic knowledge graph derived from HPD violation data.

%%RULES%%
Methodology

This view aggregates pre-computed PHC-001 (Persistent Hazardous Conditions) claims by borough. PHC-001 is evaluated at the building level by a separate pipeline; this query counts how many PHC-flagged buildings exist per borough and how many residential units they contain.

Geographic Resolution

Current resolution is borough-level only. Building nodes contain latitude and longitude fields which could support finer-grained spatial clustering (e.g., by community district or NTA) in a future version. Borough boundaries are as recorded in HPD registration data.

Epistemic Note

PHC counts reflect the state of the Watchline graph at last build. Buildings without HPD violation history (e.g., newer buildings or those with only DOB violations) are not included. The PHC-001 threshold is Watchline editorial judgment, not a statutory enforcement classification.
